Main Street Quilt and Thread Guild, a Round Rock, TX area quilt guild, is dedicated to fostering the appreciation of quilts, quilting and the other fiber arts among both quilters and the public, and to provide an encouraging environment in which fiber artists can practice and exchange ideas.
